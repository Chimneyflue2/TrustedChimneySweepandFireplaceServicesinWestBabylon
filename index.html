Fireplace and Chimney Boiler Video Inspection

Our advanced fireplace and chimney boiler video inspection service uses high-definition cameras to examine every inch of your chimney interior. This expert chimney inspection method reveals cracks, spalling, blockages, and hidden creosote deposits that standard visual inspection cannot detect. A detailed video report is provided after every inspection, giving you a clear picture of your chimney's condition. Video inspection is the most accurate chimney inspection method available and is highly recommended before purchasing a home or after a chimney fire.

Creosote Removal

Creosote is the number one cause of chimney fires in the United States. This highly flammable substance accumulates in layers inside your flue every time you burn wood. Our professional creosote removal service safely eliminates all three stages of creosote buildup using industry-approved techniques and specialized tools. Preventing dangerous creosote buildup through regular chimney cleaning is the single most important thing you can do to protect your home from chimney fires. Our trusted chimney sweep team in West Babylon handles even the most severe creosote cases.

Schedule Creosote Removal

Chimney Cap Installation

A quality chimney cap is one of the most cost-effective ways to protect your chimney from moisture, nesting animals, leaves, and downdraft. Our chimney cap installation service includes selecting the right cap size and material for your chimney, professional fitting, and secure mounting to withstand harsh weather. We work with stainless steel, galvanized, and copper caps to suit every chimney type and budget. Proper cap installation also helps prevent sparks from escaping, adding an extra layer of fire safety for your home in West Babylon, NY.
